About Us

UMT E-Latihan System is a virtual platform designed to deliver training and educational content online. It is a type of distance learning that enables users to remotely access learning resources and training materials over the internet. This platform can be used to provide training and professional development opportunities for faculty and staff in university. This system offers university staff a convenient and adaptable way to learn and grow their skill sets regardless of their location or schedule.
